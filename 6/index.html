Loss

$$l = \sum_{t}{{(v_{\theta}(s_t) - z_t)}^2} - {\vec{\pi}}_t \cdot \log{(\vec{p}_\theta{(s_t)})}$$

$v_{\theta}(s)$, $\vec{p}_\theta{(s)}$: Output of NN

$z_t$: Final reward of the episode (1, 0 or -1)

${\vec{\pi}}_t$: Estimate of the policy
